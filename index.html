02 // Neurological Mechanism

Why This Actually Works

[METHOD_01]

Dopamine via Intentional Completion

Neurodivergent and overwhelmed brains run short on dopamine, driving the urge to constantly switch tasks. Completing a highly structured, low-friction physical task triggers an immediate completion loop, rewarding your brain and rebuilding execution confidence.

[METHOD_02]

The Hyperfocus Side Door

Tracing intricate, hand-drawn lines requires continuous hand-eye coordination. This structural visual interaction forces the brain to engage its Task-Positive Network (the active attention system) while quieting the internal chatter of the Default Mode Network. It allows your nervous system to enter a state of safe, grounded focus without requiring forced willpower.

You aren't just tracing lines. You are building physical proof that your attention is yours to command.
